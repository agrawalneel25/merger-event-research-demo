Consideration

Helix Holdings Ltd has agreed to acquire Northbridge Software plc by scheme of arrangement. Northbridge shareholders will receive 620 pence in cash for each Northbridge share. The acquisition implies an equity value of approximately GBP 1.24 billion.

Source: offer_announcement, Offer Terms, 2026-06-03

Outstanding Condition

Clearance from the Competition and Markets Authority remains outstanding. The current Phase 1 statutory deadline is 15 September 2026. Helix stated that it continues to engage constructively with the authority.

Source: regulatory_update, Competition Review, 2026-07-18

Long-Stop Date

The long-stop date is 15 December 2026. If the scheme has not become effective by that date, the acquisition will lapse unless Helix and Northbridge agree an extension with the required consent.

Source: scheme_document, Long Stop Date, 2026-06-30

Financing

Helix confirms that committed financing is available to satisfy the cash consideration in full. The acquisition is not conditional on financing.

Source: financing_confirmation, Financing, 2026-07-02
